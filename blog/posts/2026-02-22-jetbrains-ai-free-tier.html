JetBrains AI Goes Free: A Game-Changer for Small Business Development

This week, JetBrains made a announcement that's reverberating through the developer community: AI-powered coding assistance is now free in all JetBrains IDEs.

Starting with version 2025.1, every JetBrains IDE license—including the free Community editions—includes a completely free tier of their AI tools. This isn't a limited-time trial or a stripped-down version. It's unlimited code completion, local AI access, and credit-based cloud AI features available to every developer, startup, and small business.

The Big News

JetBrains AI Free Tier: Unlimited code completion + Local AI models + Credit-based cloud features + 30 days of AI Pro access. All included with every IDE license.

What You Get for Free

1. Unlimited Code Completion

AI-powered code completion that understands your context, suggests entire functions, and learns from your coding style. No limits on usage—write as much code as you need.

2. Local AI Models

Run AI models directly on your machine. This means faster responses, better privacy (your code never leaves your computer), and no dependency on internet connectivity for basic AI assistance.

3. Cloud AI Features (Credit-Based)

Access more powerful cloud-hosted models for complex tasks. You get a monthly credit allowance that refreshes—enough for serious development work without hitting paywalls.

4. 30 Days of AI Pro Access

Every license includes a 30-day trial of the AI Pro tier, which gives you higher usage limits and access to advanced features like the new Junie coding agent.

Meet Junie: The New AI Coding Agent

Alongside the free tier announcement, JetBrains publicly released Junie, their new AI coding agent. Unlike traditional code completion that suggests snippets, Junie can execute more complex tasks:

  • Refactor entire code blocks while preserving functionality
  • Generate test suites from existing code
  • Debug complex issues by analyzing error patterns
  • Write documentation based on your implementation
  • Handle multi-step refactoring operations

Junie represents a new category of AI tool—not just predictive typing, but an actual coding assistant that can understand and execute developer intent.

The democratization of AI development tools has arrived. What was once enterprise-only technology is now accessible to solo developers and small businesses at zero cost.

What This Means for Small Businesses

Lower Development Costs

AI-assisted development typically reduces coding time by 30-50%. With JetBrains AI now free, small businesses get these efficiency gains without paying additional subscription fees. A single developer can accomplish what previously required 1.5 to 2 people.

Faster Onboarding for Junior Developers

AI tools act like a senior pair programmer sitting next to every junior dev. They suggest best practices, catch bugs before runtime, and explain unfamiliar code. This means your team can become productive faster, reducing training costs.

Better Code Quality

AI doesn't just write code faster—it writes better code. It catches edge cases, suggests optimizations, and follows consistent patterns. The result? Fewer bugs in production, less maintenance overhead, and happier customers.

Competitive Parity with Larger Companies

Enterprise companies have been using AI development tools for years. Now, the same technology is available to small businesses at no additional cost. The playing field has leveled.

How to Get Started

Step 1: Update Your JetBrains IDE

Make sure you're running version 2025.1 or later. The AI features are automatically included—no separate download required.

Step 2: Enable AI Features

Go to Settings → Tools → JetBrains AI and enable the AI assistant. You'll need to sign in with a JetBrains account (free to create) to activate your free tier.

Step 3: Configure Your Preferences

  • Choose between local and cloud AI models based on your needs
  • Set keyboard shortcuts for quick AI access
  • Customize which AI features you want active

Step 4: Start Coding

Begin using AI completion as you write code. The more you use it, the better it understands your patterns and preferences.

Paid Tiers: When to Upgrade

While the free tier is generous, heavy users or teams might consider upgrading:

Tier Price Best For
AI Free Included with IDE Individual developers, small teams, occasional AI use
AI Pro $10/month Full-time developers, power users, heavy AI workflows
AI Ultimate $20/month Teams, enterprise features, highest usage limits

The Bigger Picture: AI Development in 2026

This announcement reflects a broader trend in the software development industry: AI is no longer a premium add-on—it's becoming a standard feature.

Earlier this week, JetBrains' competitor TRAE also announced going fully free. The message is clear: The race is no longer about who has AI features—it's about who implements them best.

For small businesses, this is fantastic news. The barrier to entry for high-quality development has dropped significantly. You can build sophisticated applications faster, with fewer resources, and compete directly with companies that have much larger budgets.

Common Questions

"Is this really free forever?"

Yes. JetBrains has confirmed the AI Free tier is a permanent feature, not a limited-time promotion. It's included with every IDE license.

"What about privacy with cloud AI?"

That's why local AI models are included in the free tier. You can run everything on your own machine for complete privacy. Cloud features are optional.

"Do I need a paid JetBrains license?"

No. The free Community editions (IntelliJ IDEA Community, PyCharm Community, etc.) include the AI Free tier. Even completely free IDEs now have AI.

"Will this replace developers?"

No. AI tools are assistants, not replacements. They handle repetitive tasks and suggest improvements, but human judgment, architecture decisions, and problem-solving remain critical.

Bottom Line

JetBrains' move to include AI for free is more than a feature announcement—it's a statement about where software development is headed. AI-assisted coding is now standard, accessible, and cost-effective.

For small businesses and startups, this levels the playing field. You no longer need enterprise budgets to access enterprise-grade development tools. The future of development is here, and it's free.

The question isn't whether you should use AI development tools anymore—it's whether you can afford not to.
