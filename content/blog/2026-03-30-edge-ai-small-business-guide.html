What Edge AI Actually Means

No More Internet Required

Edge AI runs AI models directly on your devices:

  • Laptops with local processing
  • Smartphones with NPUs (Neural Processing Units)
  • IoT devices with built-in AI
  • Even your browser can now run sophisticated models

Small Language Models (SLMs) - The Game Changer

What Are SLMs?

SLMs are like their giant cousins (GPT-4, Claude 3), but:

  • Smaller: 1-10 billion parameters vs 100-700 billion
  • Faster: Run on consumer hardware
  • Cheaper: No API costs
  • Private: No data sent to third parties

The Reality Check: What Edge AI Can't Do (Yet)

Limitations to Understand

  • Complex Reasoning: Still struggles with deep analysis
  • Multimodal: Limited image/video processing
  • Real-time Updates: Can't access latest knowledge
  • Large Scale: Better for individual users than teams

When to Stick with Cloud AI

  • Complex data analysis
  • Real-time information needs
  • Large team collaboration
  • Advanced image/video processing
