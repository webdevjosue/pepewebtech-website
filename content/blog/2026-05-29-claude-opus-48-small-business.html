The Catch (There's Always One)

Opus 4.8 is incredible at generating code. It is not incredible at understanding your business.

AI doesn't know that your Laguna Beach boutique needs a different checkout flow than your Costa Mesa auto shop. It doesn't understand that your Santa Ana restaurant's reservation system has to handle walk-ins differently on weekends.

That's where human expertise still matters — and probably always will.

The sweet spot in 2026 is AI-assisted human development: an experienced developer who uses Opus 4.8 and similar tools to build faster, test better, and pass the savings to you — while still bringing real judgment to the table.
